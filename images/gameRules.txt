Object of the Game
Watch to board carefully.
Game starts when Player 1 says: "Taco" and displays their card for all to see.
Player 2 says: "Cat" and displays their card
Player 3 says: "Goat" ...
Player 4 says: "Cheese" ...
Player 5 says: "Pizza" ...
When you see a player announce a card's name that then matches the card displayed in the middle. SLAP!
As long as you aren't last, you won't collect up all the cards in the pile.
REMEMBER:
Get rid of all your cards and don't be the last to slap on a match.
